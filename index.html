Welcome to Thirty2give: A Virtual Engagement Platform

"Pay it Forward by Paying it Back"

Platform Benefits

Improve your engagement with our virtual engagement app and increase connectivity whether you are a small to medium business, educational institution or a non-profit organization with remote employees

Remote Engagement 24/7/365

Our mobile app provides unlimited access for your employees or members to engage and feel connected to your organization.

NO IT resources required

Our closed app is safe, secure and requires no setup or maintenance by your IT staff. An all-in-one platform with seamless user interface to make your employee engagement easy and hassle-free.

Connect seamlessly

Employee to leader, peer to peer, mentor to mentee, or advisor to protégé, make every communication seamless with Thirty2give’s user friendly and secure app.

Model flexibility

You choose how the relationship is established. Clients may choose to have users connect through an administrator or self select by querying the user database.

You control access

The Thirty2give administrative portal will give the businesses full control to link, add or remove users to promote the establishment of powerful mentoring/leader communication trees.

Visibility

Collect statistics and ratings to gauge the health of your relationship/engagement program to continuously grow and improve your business.

Connect globally

Stay connected to a mentor or mentee and communicate at a time that best suits your life schedule anywhere, anytime.

FAQs

Only 36M Baby Boomers are left in the US workplace and 76M Millennials are in the workplace, many seeking mentors. Because of limited access and the shortfall, mentors are forced to be highly selective in who they choose to mentor. Unconscious bias and other factors inadvertently make it even more challenging to secure a mentor. Anonymity levels the playing field giving more people a fair shot at aligning with a mentor. The other benefit of anonymity is that people that normally avoid exposure because of the situation (celebrities, politicians, disabled, etc.) but have a desire to give back can share their knowledge in a safe environment.
